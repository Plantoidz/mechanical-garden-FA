The corrupt beast that is the Yugen City Corporate world has finally eaten itself. Amidst a mysterious high profile murder, the city falls under new rule and with it comes cruel violence. Murder turns rampant, the streets feel cursed and rumours spread of paranormal deaths among the rich. Yugen’s elite fear the imminent realisation that they are now pawns in the shadow of their new tyrannical leader, and their grip on a once compliant metropolis has thus escaped them. Unbeknown to the chaos swallowing the city, Oddball plans its rescue from afar. With the whole cities truth willingly carried by his single soul, his life long plan to cure this nest of evil is cut short by the only woman he’s ever loved found at the centre of it all…
